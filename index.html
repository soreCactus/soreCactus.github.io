Dungeons of Destiny

Three heroes fight off foes in a castle.

-Arrow keys to move. -when combat is initiated, the heroes' and the enemy's action bars will charge. -when your enemy's action bar is almost full, he will attack, and an icon will pop up next to the hero he is attacking. -block the rogue, wizard, and knight with "p", "l", and "," respectively. -If you have enough action charge, attack using the rogue, wizard, and knight using "o", "k", and "m". -both blocking and attacking cost charge, and there is a penalty for attempting an action without enough charge.
